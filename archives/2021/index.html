About me

I am Yifan Shen, a PhD student in Industrial and Systems Engineering at the University of Washington, Seattle, specializing in transportation marketplace design. My research focuses on pricing and matching policies for shared rides, leveraging theoretical modeling and computational experiments to optimize efficiency and profitability in transportation systems. My research is advised by Chiwei Yan and Julia Yan.

Before joining ISE, I obtained my master’s degree in logistics engineering and bachelor’s degree in transportation at Shanghai Jiao Tong University.
