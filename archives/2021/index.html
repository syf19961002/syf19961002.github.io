About me

Yifan Shen is a PhD student in Industrial and Systems Engineering at the University of Washington, Seattle, specializing in transportation marketplace design. My research focuses on pricing and matching policies for shared rides, leveraging theoretical modeling and computational experiments to optimize efficiency and profitability in transportation systems. My research is advised by Chiwei Yan and Julia Yan.
